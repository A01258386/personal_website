I have some SMART goals.

SMART goals stand for:

Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant and Timely.

For example, my SMART goals are:

Specific: I want to graduate from BCIT in 2022 with an International Computer Information Technology Diploma.

Measurable: I want to get a diploma with an average of 85 or more

Achievable: Things I could do: attend all lectures and labs, aim for more than 85 marks for every course, attempt all assignments and homework, contribute to teamwork activities by researching and editing the writing of my international business project.

Relevant: I want to dedicate the time to my learnings so that I can fully understand and apply the theories, concepts, and applications that I have learnt

Timely: I will graduate one year and eight months from now
